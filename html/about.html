Where it all started

For five generations, we have been breeding sheep. The herd is part of the family and we tend to all of their special sheep-needs and harvest their precious wool. We put great pride in our craft and care about our herd with as much love as the human heart can offer. The harvested wool gets treated with skilled hands, knowledge passed down between generations. All natural and 100% hand made.
