Sustainable yarn produced from Hebridean sheep wool

When you get our product, a piece of the harsh Hebridean nature follows.

Our goal is to provide the highest quality yarn to both retailers and consumers directly. Following the cultural heritage of hand-crafted, hand-dyed wool with just natural and locally produced materials.

Sticking to tradition also means using old tools for harvesting wool and producing yarn. It's hard work, but when we see the result, it's all worth it! Our sheep eats nutritios Hebridean grass every day during summer, and every single one of them is treasured deeply.

If you care about quality, sustainability and animal welfare, this is the product for you!
