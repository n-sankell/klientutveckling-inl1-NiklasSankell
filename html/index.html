Sustainible yarn production from Hebridean sheep

When you get our product, a piece of the harsh Hebridean nature follows.

Our goal is to provide the highest quality yarn to both retailers and consumers directly. Following the cultural heritage of hand-crafted, hand-dyed wool with just natural and locally produced materials.
